ESAD.CR Events App

  • UX Design

  • UI Design

  • Figma

  • 2023

The ESAD.CR Events App is a mobile-first concept developed in Figma to streamline how the ESAD.CR community engages with the school’s rich calendar of cultural and academic events. Designed for both students and professors, the app offers an intuitive and visually appealing interface that makes it easy to discover, save, and plan for exhibitions, lectures, performances, workshops, and more — all in one place. With clear navigation and flexible filters, users can explore events by date, category, or department. Each event page provides detailed information including time, location, and descriptions, along with options to bookmark, share, or set personal reminders. The app’s clean design reflects the creative identity of ESAD.CR while prioritizing ease of use and accessibility. Built to connect the entire school community, the ESAD.CR Events App goes beyond simple scheduling — it serves as a digital hub that fosters engagement, inspiration, and participation across disciplines.
